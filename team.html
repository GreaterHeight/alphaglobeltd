Our team

The people behind Alpha Globe.

Every person we support is cared for by a small, consistent team — not a rotating cast of strangers. Here's how our team is structured.

How our teams are structured

Small, consistent teams around every person we support

Every home is led by a registered manager and staffed by a core team who get to know each person well — because trust is built through familiar faces, not rotating shifts.

Registered Manager

Service leadership & compliance

Holds a relevant Level 5 Diploma in Leadership for Health and Social Care, and is responsible for the day-to-day quality, safety, and regulatory compliance of the service.

Deputy Manager

Day-to-day operations

Supports the registered manager with rota management, staff supervision, and ensuring support plans are followed and reviewed on schedule.

Positive Behaviour Support Lead

Behaviour & risk specialist

Trained in positive behaviour support (PBS) frameworks, leading functional assessments and de-escalation training across the staff team.

Senior Support Workers

Shift leadership

Experienced support workers who lead shifts, mentor newer staff, and act as key workers for individual service users.

Support Workers

Day-to-day support

The consistent, familiar faces who provide daily support — from morning routines to community activities and everything between.

Compliance & Quality Lead

Governance & safeguarding

Oversees policy compliance, staff training records, safeguarding processes, and supports external inspections and audits.

Safer recruitment

Every team member is vetted, trained, and supervised.

All Alpha Globe staff undergo enhanced DBS checks, reference verification, and structured induction before working unsupervised. Ongoing training covers safeguarding, positive behaviour support, mental health awareness, first aid, and medication administration, refreshed on a regular cycle.

  • Enhanced DBS check for every member of staff
  • Structured induction aligned to the Care Certificate standards
  • Regular supervision and annual appraisal
  • Ongoing mandatory and specialist training
