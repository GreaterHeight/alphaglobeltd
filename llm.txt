# Alpha Globe Ltd

> Alpha Globe Ltd is a registered supported living care provider in England, delivering person-centred support for adults with high to complex mental health needs. Our aim is to help the people we support move toward positive contribution, achievement, and self-actualisation as adults.

Alpha Globe operates supported living services across Kent and Greater London, staffed by trained, DBS-checked support workers under the leadership of registered managers. Support is built around six core disciplines: mental health support, behaviour support, community access, risk management, 24/7 support, and domestic support.
