About Alpha Globe

Support that starts with who you are, not what's on file.

Alpha Globe Ltd is a registered supported living care provider delivering person-centred support for vulnerable adults with high to complex mental health needs — helping people move toward positive contribution, achievement, and self-actualisation.

Our story

Built by people who believe support should open doors, not manage decline.

Alpha Globe was founded on a simple observation: too many adults with complex mental health needs are offered support that keeps them safe but keeps them stuck. We set out to build something different — supported living that treats safety and growth as the same goal, not competing priorities.

Today, our team works alongside adults with high to complex mental health needs across Kent and Greater London, in homes designed to feel like home, with staff trained to walk alongside people rather than manage them.

Our mission

Enjoy. Achieve. Contribute. Self-actualise.

Our mission is to deliver person-centred support for vulnerable adults with high to complex mental health needs, effecting a positive change in their lives — eventually empowering them to go on to make a positive contribution, enjoy and achieve, and ultimately to self-actualise as adults.

How we work

Our person-centred approach

A consistent framework, applied individually to every person we support.

Get to know the person

Referral and transition begin with listening — to the individual, their family, and the professionals who already know them.

Build the plan together

Support plans are co-produced, reviewed regularly, and adjusted as goals, needs, and confidence change.

Grow toward independence

Every plan includes a pathway — skills, community connections, and steps toward greater self-direction.

Quality & safeguarding

Regulated, accountable, and always improving.

Alpha Globe Ltd operates as a registered supported living provider, subject to regulatory oversight appropriate to our services. We maintain robust safeguarding policies, staff vetting (including enhanced DBS checks), regular training, and open channels for feedback and complaints from the people we support, their families, and referring professionals.
