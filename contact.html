In an emergency, please don't wait for a reply here

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 999. For urgent mental health support that isn't life-threatening, call NHS 111 (option 2 in England), or the Samaritans free on 116 123, available 24/7. Existing service users and families can also reach our 24/7 on-call line — see your care plan for the direct number.
